# Virtual Pet Game

A fun, interactive Tomagotchi-style virtual pet game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## About

Welcome to your very own virtual pet! This interactive game lets you take care of a digital companion by feeding it, playing with it, and keeping it clean. Watch as your pet's mood changes based on your actions, and make sure to keep its hunger, happiness, and health levels up to prevent it from getting sick or dying.

## Features

- **Interactive Pet**: A cute virtual pet that responds to your care
- **Three Main Activities**:
  - Feed: Increases hunger but decreases happiness
  - Play: Increases happiness but decreases hunger
  - Clean: Increases health but slightly decreases happiness
- **Sleep Function**: Toggle the pet's sleep state
- **Visual Feedback**: Emoji-based mood representation
- **Status Messages**: See what your pet is doing in real-time
- **Game Over Conditions**: When any stat reaches zero, your pet dies
- **Automatic Stats Decay**: Stats decrease gradually over time

## How to Play

1. Click the "Feed" button to give your pet food
2. Click the "Play" button to have fun with your pet
3. Click the "Clean" button to clean your pet
4. Click "Sleep" to put your pet to rest or wake it up
5. Watch as your pet's stats change based on your actions
6. Try to keep all stats above zero to keep your pet alive!

## Game Mechanics

- **Hunger**: Decreases over time and when playing
- **Happiness**: Decreases over time and when feeding
- **Health**: Decreases over time and when playing
- **Sleep State**: When sleeping, the pet doesn't lose stats

## Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
